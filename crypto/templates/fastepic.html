What is fastepic.eu

It's place when everyone can start using Epic-Cash just with an email address. Thanks to unique characteristics of MimbleWimble protocol, you can trade Epics without giving anyone your personal data. With fastepic.eu you can:
  • Store Epics secure on your online wallet
    •
  • Send coins to anyone in many different ways
    •
  • Receive coins from anyone and anywhere
  • Buy things for Epics without any 3rd party
    •
  • Buy or sell coins for Bitcoin with integrated exchange
    •
  • Mine Epic with your hardware without any configurations

    • How to start

    It's important for us to make you feel comfortable with using fastepic services. To get direct help with first steps, please join official Telegram Epic-Cash and ask for help @PinsertIco or @blacktyg3r - we are there for you to help.

    In order to create account you will need to use an email address (fastepic team recommend using Protonmail due to strong secure and privacy, but you can use any email you want) and send some coins there - account and wallet will be automatically created after receive funds. To achive this you can:

    Use CLI Wallet
    Send coins from your Official CLI Wallet typing in command line:

    epic-wallet send -d https://fastepic.eu/youremail 0.001

    Where 0.001 is recommended amount - you can use any,
    and youremail is your full email address.

    Withdraw From Exchange
    If you have your coins on exchange like Citex or BiHodl, you can withdraw from there directly using address:

    http://fastepic.eu:13415/youremail

    Ask For Help
    Epic-Cash is a community project, there are many people to help you start your journey! Just ask someone to send a small amount to your new account. Talk to us at Telegram or Discord.
    Start Mining
    After start mining with fastepic pool coins from pool will be automatically send to your main account.
    Send email
    You can simply just send an email to cash@fastepic.eu with title "give me account please" to get your account created.

    From now you can get access to your fastepic account by using email address at fastepic.eu. New password is created and sent to you for each login attempt.

    At this point you are ready to start trade your Epic-Cash for bitcoin and other way round by using same email address at Epic Home Exchange. You can also send there your bitcoins, to buy more precious Epics :).
