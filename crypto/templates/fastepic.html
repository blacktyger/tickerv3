What is fastepic.eu

Primary goal for fastepic.eu team is to deliver secure and anonymous place where you can mine and trade your Epic-Cash. Opposite to other exchanges there is no need for registration, KYC or giving any personal data. Creating account is easy, and everyone can use it straight away!
  • Trade EPIC-BTC pair in secure and advanced exchange enginee
  • Send Epics to everyone just using email address or SMS
  • Receive Epics from anyone without any extra software via email or SMS
  • Mine using simple software for Windows without configuration
  • Store safely your Epics without installing any extra software

    • How to start

    In order to create your account you simply need to initiate it by sending small amount of Epic-Cash.

    The best way to do this is to download and configure Official CLI Wallet (click for guide), but you can also send coins from other exchange, or ask someone else to do this for you. Your address will look like this:

    https://fastepic.eu/youremail

    You can use any email address you want, but fastepic team recommend Protonmail due to strong secure and privacy.

    In order to send from your CLI Wallet just type in command line:

    epic-wallet send -d https://fastepic.eu/youremail 0.001

    Where 0.001 is recommended amount (you can use any), and youremail is your full email address.

    From now you can get access to your fastepic account by using given email address at fastepic.eu. New password is created and sent to you for each login attempt.

    At this point you are ready to start trade your Epic-Cash for bitcoin and other way round by using same email address at Epic Home Exchange. You can also send there your bitcoins, to buy more precious Epics :).
